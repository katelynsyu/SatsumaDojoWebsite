Introduction

Satsuma Dojo focusses on teaching traditional karate. This includes an emphasis on etiquette, goal attainment, and personal development which we believe lends to a way of life: karatedo. Though we focuss on Shotokan karate, we also teach Shoriniji-ryu. This traditional training in two different styles of karate simultaneously is exeedingly difficult to find. We also have the rare privilege to be a part of both SKIF-USA and SKIF-USF organizations.
